CAD Model of Submarine Toy

Skills: Reverse engineering, SolidWorks CAD, DFM/DFA, GD&T, Engineering Drawings

Course: Graphics and Design MECE E3408

Completed as a midtern project for Columbia's Graphics and Design course, this CAD submarine was reserve engineered from a Green Toy's submarine toy product.

It is composed of four distinct parts: two halves of the main body, one hatch, and one propellor wheel. The reverse engineering process required precise measurements using calipers and replication of the submarine's inner features, including the clicking lock mechanisms. Each component was carefully measured and replicated to create a high-fidelity final assembly. Deliverables included the engineering drawings, a bill of materials, and a memo summarizing my work process.
